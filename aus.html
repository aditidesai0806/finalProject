So, Here's the Deal...

Australia isn’t just a continent full of cute kangaroos and koala bears - Australian Senator Fraser Anning’s comments suggesting Muslim immigration is the reason behind recent terrorist attacks has underscored the Islamophobia present in Australia. He has referred to Islam as a religion “equivalent to fascism,” and has implied that terrorism is unequivocally associated with Islam. For the past several years, anti-Islam demonstrations have taken place throughtout Australia. One instance in Melbourne resulted in violence between anti-immigration and anti-racism rallyists, prompting the Australian police to turn to pepper spray. Multiple polls, e.g. those conducted by the company Essential and Western Sydney University, have discovered the prominent Islamophobia among the population. They have found that almost half the population supports a ban on Muslim immigration into the continent and that Muslims in Sydney experience racism at a rate three times that of other Australians, respectively.
