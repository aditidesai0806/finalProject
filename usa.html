Gun Control in the USA


So, Here's the Deal...


Gun possession is a constitutionally protected right in the USA. However, mass murders that take place with guns are extremely common. The United States has by far the highest homicide-by-firearm rate among developed countries. Supporters of increased gun control in the United States argue that limiting access to guns will save lives and reduce crime; opponents insist that it would actually do the opposite by preventing law-abiding citizens from defending themselves against armed criminals. There have been so many mass shootings, that the majority of them are never even mentioned in any media; the only ones mentioned in media are the very large shootings, and typically spark short lived debates about the need for stricter gun control.
