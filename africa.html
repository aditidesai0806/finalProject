Menstrual Health In Africa

So What's The Deal?

Girls around the world lack the basic knowledge about their sexual and reproductive health. Many struggle to access menstrual health supplies; others grapple with shame, taboos, surrounding menstruation. These fundamental issues undermine girls’ health and rights. Girls can be subjected to stigman and even miss school due to the difficulty in managing their menstrual hygiene. Many of these concerns were addressed at the recent Menstrual Health Management Symposium in Johannesburg, South Africa. It is the first such meeting which is being held in the region. It was organized by the South African Department of Women and the UNFPA. The symposium aims to break down the taboos and build positive norms around menstruation.
